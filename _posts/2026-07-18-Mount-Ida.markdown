### *So many marmots*

[Strava](https://www.strava.com/activities/19368292999)

Lovely day out with Melissa. Tons of marmots slowed our travel; hard to pass them without taking photos and admiring their elegance and grace. Had planned on dropping into the drainage below Mount Ida with all the lakes but decided to keep the day more casual to preserve Melissa's legs for her race in a week.
